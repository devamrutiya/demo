What is strong tag?

  • The HTML <strong> Tag is the parsed tag and is used to show the importance of the text.
  • The content inside it is generally in bold format.
  • The primary purpose of <strong> tag is to provide semantic meaning to the content, indicating its importance to both browsers and developers.
  • strong tag is in html 5

strong tag Syntax:

<strong>text....</strong>

strong tag Example:

This text is important!
