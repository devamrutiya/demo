blockquote tag

What is blockquote tag?

  • HTML <blockquote> tag makes long quotes look special. It gives them a different style to stand out and look better.
  • It lets you use different things like headings or lists inside the quote.
  • This helps you organize and make your quotes more interesting for people reading your webpage.

Note:

The <blockquote> tag in HTML5 specifies the section quoted from other sources, unlike HTML4.1, which defines long quotations that span multiple lines.

blockquote tag syntax:

<blockquote>text....</blockquote>
