Marquee

What is marquee tag?

  • The <marquee> tag in HTML is used to create a scrolling piece of text or an image.
  • It can scroll horizontally from left to right or right to left, and vertically from top to bottom or bottom to top.
  • It can scroll in various directions, such as left, right, up, or down.
  • marquee tag is in html 4
  • But this tag has been deprecated in HTML 5.

marquee tag Syntax:

<marquee> behavior="scroll" direction="lift">text....</marquee&>
